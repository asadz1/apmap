What are AP Exams?

Advanced Placement (AP) Exams are standardized tests administered globally by the College Board, a U.S. based non-profit company. Designed to evaluate a student’s mastery of college-level material, they exist to provide a consistent academic benchmark across countries, schools, and curricula, regardless of a student's educational background or location.

AP Map is an independent resource and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or associated with the College Board. All AP® trademarks are owned by the College Board.
While we strive to provide accurate and helpful information, university credit policies may change and often include exceptions or department-level discretion. Always verify all AP credit decisions directly with your university to ensure accuracy.
