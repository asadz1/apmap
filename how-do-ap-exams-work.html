What are AP Exams?

Advanced Placement Exams (better known as AP Exams) are global standardized tests offered by the non-profit, American company Collegeboard. They are created to serve as a baseline examination that tests students' knowledge of different subjects, regardless of what country they are in or what curriculum they are taught.
